About the Shop

A one-man shop with three rules.

Anthony has been on the wrench since 1989. Every bike in the shop gets the same treatment: documented start-to-finish with photos and video, so you see exactly what you're paying for.

Good service is never cheap, and cheap service is never good. We don't pretend otherwise — we just back it up.

Ruthless Kustomz is a working shop in Pomona that has been keeping local riders on the road for over three decades. From tune-ups and brake jobs to ground-up motor rebuilds and dyno-style performance tuning, every job leaves with a full record of what was done — photos, video, parts, the works.

We service motorcycles, dirt bikes, ATVs and scooters of every brand. If it has two wheels, four wheels, or a fuel injector, bring it in. We answer the phone day and night, run our own fuel injector bench & flow tests in-house, and offer pickup and delivery for the Pomona area.
